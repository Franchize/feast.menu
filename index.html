Feast

Eat with your eyes

A visually aesthetic gastronomical experience launching soon!


Feast is a B2B company that works with restaurants (FCD, CD, FD, non-QSR) to provide short clip montageS of menu items in a digital menu format for their customers to view prior to ordering. We pride ourselves in solving the problems and limitations many consumers experience when ordering food- the lack of quality, accessible, and sharable visuals of restaurants menu items. Our goal is to be the leader in providing Foodies with a service that enhances their food ordering experience.


Feast was created out of the need FOR useful media that showcased a restaurants menu items. Foodies and the likes WILL find it helpful to view menu items during their ordering process.
